Restaurant Description

Sydney's Seafood Kitchen is based out of downtown Atlanta, GA. Our menu is inspired by a cajun American southern seafood twang. It's a great atmosphere welcoming all seafood and soul food lovers. There's great music and great food.
Seafood lovers from all over gather for our Crab & Shrimp Grits, Deep Fried Lobster Tails, Cajun Fried Catfish & Three Cheese Macaroni, and our fans favorite Hennessy Peach Cobbler. We're open everyday 11AM until 10PM Monday through Thursday and 11AM until midnight on Friday through Sunday. Come hang out with us, listen to some great music, and enjoy great service and great food.
